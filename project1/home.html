We are: The Internet, a soul band from LA!

The Internet is an acclaimed American band that has made significant waves in the music industry with its unique blend of soul, R&B, funk, and neo-soul. Formed in Los Angeles in 2011, the band emerged as a side project of the Grammy-nominated collective Odd Future, and quickly distinguished itself with its smooth, innovative sound.

The Internet debuted with their album "Purple Naked Ladies" in 2011, which was well-received for its experimental approach and laid-back vibe. However, it was their third album, "Ego Death" (2015), that catapulted them to wider acclaim. The album earned a Grammy nomination for Best Urban Contemporary Album and showcased their evolution with standout tracks like "Girl" and "Special Affair."

The Internet continues to be a powerful force in modern soul and R&B, known for their authentic, heartfelt music and captivating live performances. With their innovative sound and dedicated fan base, The Internet is a testament to the enduring appeal of soul music in the contemporary music scene.
